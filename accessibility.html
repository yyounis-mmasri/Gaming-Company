What web accessibility is

Accessible sites allow visitors with disabilities to browse their sites with the same or similar level of ease and enjoyment as other visitors. This can be achieved with the capabilities of the system on which the site is operating, and through assistive technologies.

Accessibility adjustments on this site

We have adjusted this site in accordance with WCAG 2.0 / 2.1 / 2.2 - level relevant applied guidelines, and have made this site accessible to the level of A or AA where relevant design applies. The adjustments here have been designed for users with assistive technologies, such as screen readers and keyboard users. As part of this effort, this site has gone through the following adjustments:

  • Used the Accessibility Wizard to find and fix potential accessibility issues
  • Set the language of the site
  • Defined clear heading structures for all site pages
  • Added alt text for images
  • Adjusted contrast ratios to ensure they meet the required color contrast standards
  • Redesigned site menus for keyboard navigation
  • Ensured all videos, audio, and forms on this site are accessible
